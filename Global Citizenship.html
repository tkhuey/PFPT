Protect Our Planet
We Take Responsibility
for Our Footprint
As a cloud-native cybersecurity company, our environmental footprint is shaped by how we power our offices, operate our infrastructure, and engage our supply chain. We are committed to measuring what matters, operating responsibly, and building toward a lower-carbon future.
GHG Emissions
Measuring Our Baseline
Proofpoint is conducting its inaugural Scope 1, 2, and 3 greenhouse gas emissions inventory under the GHG Protocol framework. This baseline will serve as the foundation for all future reduction targets and decarbonization planning. Our full inventory is targeted for disclosure in June 2026 per SB 253 requirements.
Sustainable Offices
Responsible Operations
We promote environmentally responsible practices across our global offices — LED lighting, virtual collaboration tools to minimize travel emissions, waste reduction programs, recycling, certified e-waste management, and the elimination of single-use items. Our Sunnyvale headquarters is LEED Gold certified.
Cloud & Infrastructure
Sustainable Data Centers
Proofpoint partners with cloud and colocation providers committed to renewable energy and sustainable operations, including Databank and Equinix. We exclusively use Energy Star and LEED-certified facilities and prioritize infrastructure partners whose energy sourcing and efficiency standards align with our environmental commitments. Currently 25% of our datacenter usage is powered by renewable energy, with a commitment to grow that share over time.
One Tree Planted Partnership
Voluntary Environmental Action
Proofpoint is partnering with One Tree Planted to fund global reforestation through employee engagement. Our goal is a minimum of 1,000 trees planted in our inaugural year of partnership. This initiative is a voluntary environmental contribution, reported separately from our GHG inventory — not a carbon offset.
Hardware & E-Waste
Responsible Lifecycle Management
We extend the life of IT equipment where possible and ensure responsible disposal through certified e-waste recyclers. We comply with regulatory requirements at each location and are exploring buy-back, donation, and refurbishment programs to further minimize environmental impact.

Protect Our Communities
We Show Up for
Our Communities
Proofpoint Cares is our global program for giving, volunteering, and community engagement. It's where our values become actions — in the neighborhoods, cities, and countries where our people live and work.
Proofpoint Cares
Our Global Giving Program
Proofpoint Cares empowers employees to make an impact in causes that matter to them — through volunteering, charitable giving, and company-matched initiatives. We encourage community involvement, spotlight social impact stories, and provide the tools to make giving easy and measurable via Benevity.
Volunteer Time Off
Two Days, Your Causes
All full-time Proofpoint employees receive two Volunteer Days Off (VTO) annually — 16 hours to dedicate to causes they care about. Whether it's a company-sponsored event, a team volunteering day, or an independent activity, every act of service counts and is tracked toward our collective impact. In 2025, Proofpoint employees logged 1,604.5 VTO hours globally.
Global Priorities
Where We Focus
Proofpoint Cares focuses on three pillars: Protect People (community care, giving, and employee-led initiatives), Protect the Planet (sustainability and environmental action), and Protect the Future (education, innovation, and tech-for-good). These guide how we give back and where we make our greatest impact.
Giving Back Through Every Meal
One Meal Donated for Every Two Purchased
Proofpoint's weekly office lunch vendor, HUNGRY, partners with Feeding America to donate one meal for every two meals purchased, turning everyday workplace dining into a direct community impact. Since the program launched in October 2025, Proofpoint employees have generated 13,416 meals donated across 26,832 total orders through March 2026, with contributions growing each month.
One Tree Planted
Steps That Plant Trees
Our inaugural partnership with One Tree Planted ties employee steps — tracked via Wellhub — directly to tree donations. For every 500,000 steps logged globally, Proofpoint funds a tree planting. Our Year 1 goal is 1,000+ trees. Employees can also donate directly to support reforestation efforts worldwide.

Oversight & Ethics
Board-Level Accountability
Proofpoint's ESG Policy was formally approved by the Board of Directors in 2025 and is subject to annual review. An Executive Committee (CFO and CPO) oversees ESG strategy, supported by a cross-functional ESG Steering Committee spanning Legal, IT, Finance, Real Estate, People & Culture, and Communications.
Data Privacy
Protecting What Matters
Our cross-functional data privacy team maintains authorized access controls, privacy and security audits, encryption policies, breach notification procedures, and Data Processing Agreements with Standard Contractual Clauses. Live privacy training is provided to all new hires every two weeks, with annual all-company training.
Responsible AI
AI with Integrity
Proofpoint's Responsible AI framework is grounded in fairness, accountability, transparency, and security. We maintain clear governance structures and human oversight for AI-driven decisions, embed privacy controls into every stage of the AI lifecycle, and align with NIST's AI Risk Management Framework. AI enhances our mission — it doesn't replace our responsibility.
